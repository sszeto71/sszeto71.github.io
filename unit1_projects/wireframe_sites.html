Intro to Wireframing

Wireframing #1: AirBnB

  • What problem is the web page solving?

    • AirBnB solves the issue of supply and demand of available accomodations with people looking for places to stay. AirBnB has great inventory in most locations.

  • What visual challenges are there?

    • Some of the visual challenges on AirBnB home page include the following: 1) relevant accomodation content in a limited space and 2) keeping it simple to find accomodations without the need to navigate to other pages.

Wireframing #2: Uber

  • What problem is the web page solving?

    • Uber provides an alternative to taxi and other types of transportation.

  • What visual challenges are there?

    • Some of the visual challenges on Uber main app page include the following: 1) determining the right amount and size of map location for relevancy and 2) showing real-time location of available cars

Wireframing #3: Yelp

  • What problem is the web page solving?

    • Yelp provides an easy way to get recommendations from other people of local services.

  • What visual challenges are there?

    • Some of the visual challenges on Yelp home page include the following: 1) determining the right amount of relevant content being shown on the home page without overwhelming customer's user experience, 2) keeping the home page clean and simple, while being useful, and 3) determining local recommendations based on geo IP address.
